Who are we !

“ The greatest gift one can give to a child is to teach him to know and master himself with the help of good sanskar. A child’s physical, intellectual and spiritual growth requires conducive environment. ”

ACS, Established in year 2015, our education prepares students to be intelligent, innovative, to be responsible and compassionate leaders who place the welfare of others before self and who are willing to embrace the challenges of our century as opportunities to make a difference.

We believe that every child is a valued member of our school. Each child is seen as a capable, competent and active participant in his or her own learning journey. We focus on the development of the whole child, encompassing the social, emotional, spiritual, physical and cultural needs alongside the academic and intellectual progress. The driving force of what we do comes from knowing our students; their needs, characteristics and wellbeing.

Why choose us ?

We provide best facilities to the students which will help them in their development. We believe that student should enjoy learning and not force to learn anything.
